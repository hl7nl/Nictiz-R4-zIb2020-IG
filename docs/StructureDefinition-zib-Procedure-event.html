Resource Profile: zib Procedure event ( Abstract )

Official URL: http://nictiz.nl/fhir/StructureDefinition/zib-Procedure-event Version: 0.9.0
Draft as of 2023-10-17 Computable Name: ZibProcedureevent

Copyright/Legal: Copyright and related rights waived via CC0, https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/. Therapeutic or diagnostic procedure undergone by the patient (the complementary profile zib-Procedure-request can be used for therapeutic or diagnostic procedures that the patient will undergo). A procedure can be a simple blood pressure measurement, but also a complex heart surgery. In some care settings, the term treatment is used instead of the term procedure.

This Procedure resource represents the Dutch zib (‘Zorginformatiebouwsteen’, i.e. Health and Care Information Model) Procedure v5.2 (2020).

Usage:

  • Derived from this Resource Profile: nl core Procedure event
  • Refer to this Resource Profile: zib Encounter and zib MedicalDevice
